The Social Tracking skill manages continuous tracking across all four platforms. Tracked items are monitored regularly by Xpoz, resulting in better data coverage and fresher results.

## Why use continuous tracking?

- **Better coverage**: Regular monitoring captures more posts and updates, so your results are more complete.
- **Fresher data**: Tracked items are kept up to date, so you always get the most recent content.

## Supported Types

| Platform    | Keyword | User | Subreddit | Hashtag |
| ----------- | ------- | ---- | --------- | ------- |
| Twitter/X   | Yes     | Yes  | -         | -       |
| Instagram   | Yes     | Yes  | -         | -       |
| Reddit      | Yes     | Yes  | Yes       | -       |
| TikTok      | Yes     | Yes  | -         | Yes     |

## Xpoz Tools Used

| Tool                 | Purpose                         |
| -------------------- | ------------------------------- |
| `getTrackedItems`    | List all currently tracked items |
| `addTrackedItems`    | Add new items to track           |
| `removeTrackedItems` | Stop tracking items              |

## Common Workflows

### Track your brand across all platforms

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add brand keywords">
    Add your brand name as a keyword on Twitter, Instagram, Reddit, and TikTok.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track official accounts">
    Add your official accounts as tracked users on each platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track relevant subreddits">
    Add any relevant subreddits where your brand is discussed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Monitor a competitor

<Steps>
  <Step title="Track competitor keywords">
    Track the competitor's brand name as a keyword across platforms.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track competitor accounts">
    Track their official social media accounts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run competitive analysis">
    Periodically run the [Competitive Intelligence](/skills/competitive-intel) skill to compare metrics.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### TikTok hashtag campaign

<Steps>
  <Step title="Track the campaign hashtag">
    Track the campaign hashtag on TikTok.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track related keywords">
    Track related keywords to capture posts that mention the campaign without the hashtag.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor performance">
    Monitor post volume and engagement over time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Subreddit monitoring

<Steps>
  <Step title="Track the subreddit">
    Track the subreddit by name on Reddit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track cross-subreddit keywords">
    Track relevant keywords to capture cross-subreddit discussions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Analyze periodically">
    Use the [Reddit Research](/skills/reddit-research) skill for periodic analysis.
  </Step>
</Steps>
